Our Services

Strategy Planning

Spica Corp advises, formulates, and plans with you to build the proper infrastructure and logical environments tailored to your needs. We strategize and employ the latest methodologies to develop efficient software and to reduce pertinent development costs. We analyze and advise based on your solutions requirements, focusing on creative problem solving and planning.

Data Migration

With little to no downtime, Spica Corp performs data migrations from legacy relational databases and other sources (e.g., SQL Server, DB2, Oracle, PostgreSQL, etc.) to MongoDB. With a wide array of resources and experience in data migrations, our in-house team of professionals will speed and optimize your migration, ensuring smooth performance and proper implementation of your data.

Scaling and Deployment

The team of expert consultants at Spica Corp work to optimize your platform with scale, speed, and security of applications and databases in mind. In reviewing your organization’s needs, we place emphasis on three key metrics: cluster scaling; performance scaling; and data scaling. With our approach, we improve data throughput and functionality by distributing your database through many nodes, maintaining 100k+ database reads/write per second and strict latency SLAs, while stowing billions of documents in the database.

Backup and Disaster Recovery

With automated, efficient protection and granular recovery, Spica Corp helps you reduce costs, improve performance, and lessen risk. When dealing with large data sets, it’s important to understand and avert potential pitfalls. Spica Corp utilizes a muti-layered approach to protect your data. We use multiple data centers to act as an additional layer of security and safeguard. Additionally, we store data on various layers — file systems, databases, or shards (part of the cluster) — to certify proper protection and recovery states.

Real-time Analytics Support

Spica Corp delivers organizations with the proper tools to monitor, visualize, and report complex datasets easily. We provide completely customized MongoDB dashboards that include metrics, filters, visualizations, and robust reporting. Spica Corp can analyze data directly within the database, providing you with real-time results without the costly data warehouse loads.

Developer Training

Spica Corp provides developer training to candidates of various levels of skill. Our instructors equip candidates with industry expertise on MongoDB to enhance their knowledge and confidence. Course contents include: data modeling, indexing and aggregation, query and sharding, ingestion, and data replication, and cluster operations administration.
